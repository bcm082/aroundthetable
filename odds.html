How to Read the Odds

Positive Odds (+150)

The underdog. A +150 means you win $150 for every $100 bet. These are the teams you can pick for Around the Table.

Negative Odds (-200)

The favorite. A -200 means you need to bet $200 to win $100. You cannot pick these teams in our game.

Point Spread

The predicted margin of victory. A team with +7.5 is expected to lose by 7.5 points or less.

Live Updates

Odds change throughout the week based on betting action and team news. Check back regularly for the latest lines.
